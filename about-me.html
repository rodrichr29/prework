My Background


So I was born in Jan 29, 2003 at Fort Worth, Texas. I have grew up in Fort Worth till this day, and I had always been a weird kid during my childhood, like being a loner, an early foodie, a daydreamer, and even befriended a bully. My most recalled memory of my chilhood was the aspiration to become an artist. I really enjoyed art at that time because I would always be instructed to draw in a step by step fashion by my art teacher at the school, I truly liked the idea of inputing certain instructions to get an output. Sadly, I would later realize that I do not have the creative thinking that I would make myself to believe, and I dropped my aspiration of art. It wouldn't till later in highschool that I found programming.
